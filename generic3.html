Assignment 3

Tableau Map

Final Wealth Distribution of Billionaires by Country and industries

Overview:

This assignment aims to visualize statistics on the world's billionaires across various industries. The goal is to provide insights into the distribution of wealth and the business sectors of billionaires worldwide. To achieve this, I used the “Billionaires Statistics Dataset”.

1. Sum of Final Worth Distribution by Country:

This map illustrates the total sum of final worth by country. The darker the shade of red, the higher the sum of final worth in that country. However, lighter shades of red represent countries with lower sum of final worth. This allows for a quick visual comparison of wealth distribution on a global scale.

Interactive Hover Effect:

Hovering over a specific country on the map reveals a tooltip displaying:

Country Name: Identifies the country you've hovered over.

Final Worth (USD): Shows the exact sum of final worth concentrated within that particular country.

This interactive element allows for a more precise understanding of wealth distribution across different regions. By hovering over individual countries, users can obtain the exact figures in USD, facilitating a more detailed analysis of which countries hold the highest concentrations of billionaire wealth.

2. Final Worth Distribution Across Industries by Country

This stacked bar chart illustrates the distribution of final worth across various industries by country. Each bar represents a country, and within each bar, different colors signify different industries. The length of each segment within the bar indicates the total final worth contributed by that industry in the respective country. The legend helps identify the industries represented by the colors.

Hovering over a specific segment within a bar reveals a tooltip displaying:

Country Name: Identifies the country associated with the bar.

Industry Name: Specifies the industry represented by the color of that segment.

This interactive element allows for a more detailed and insightful exploration of the data. By this we can easily compare not only the relative contribution of each industry within a country but also the absolute values (in USD) associated with each segment. This additional information helps paint a clearer picture of how wealth is distributed across industries and their economic impact on a national level.

Final Worth (USD): Provides the exact sum of final worth contributed by that specific industry in that particular country.

3. Unveiling Billionaires' Wealth by Industry

This square graph offers a unique perspective on the final worth distribution among billionaires categorized by industry. Each square represents an individual billionaire, and its color denotes their primary industry affiliation (refer to the legend for color-coding details).

Interactive Features:

Legend: Located on the graph, the legend acts as a key, identifying the industries associated with each color used for the squares. This ensures clear understanding of the color scheme.

Final Worth Filter (Optional): This filter allows users to explore specific wealth ranges. By adjusting the filter, you can focus on billionaires within a chosen financial bracket.

Hovering over a specific square reveals a tooltip displaying:

Billionaire Name/Person name: Identifies the individual billionaire represented by the square.

Final Worth (USD): Shows the exact final worth of that particular billionaire.

Industry: Specifies the industry the billionaire is primarily associated with (based on the square's color).

This visualization provides a clear comparison of wealth distribution among billionaires across industries, with the size of the squares directly reflecting their final worth. You can gain insights into industry trends and identify prominent figures within each sector, while the interactive elements enhance the exploration by offering additional details and potential filtering capabilities.
