Assignment 3

Tableau Map

Overview:

This assignment aims to visualize statistics on the world's billionaires across various industries. The goal is to provide insights into the distribution of wealth and the business sectors of billionaires worldwide. To achieve this, I used the “Billionaires Statistics Dataset”.

1. Sum of Final Worth Distribution by Country:

This map illustrates the total sum of final worth by country. The darker the shade of red, the higher the total worth in that country. Conversely, lighter shades of red represent countries with lower total worth. By hovering over each country on the map, you can see detailed information, including the country name and the exact sum of final worth in USD. This visualization helps identify which countries have the highest concentrations of billionaire wealth.

2. Final Worth Distribution Across Industries by Country

This stacked bar chart illustrates the distribution of final worth across various industries by country. Each bar represents a country, and within each bar, different colors signify different industries. The length of each segment within the bar indicates the total final worth contributed by that industry in the respective country. The legend helps identify the industries represented by the colors. This visualization allows for an easy comparison of how wealth is distributed across industries within each country, highlighting the dominant sectors and their economic impact on a national level.

3. Final Worth of Billionaires by Industry

This square graph showcases the final worth of billionaires categorized by industry. Each square represents a billionaire, with the size of the square indicating their final worth. Different colors are used to distinguish between industries, making it easy to see which sectors each billionaire is associated with. The legend on the graph helps to identify the industries represented by the various colors. This visualization provides a clear comparison of the wealth distribution among billionaires across different industries, highlighting the most lucrative sectors.
